Chopsticks Holder

A simple chopsticks holder to keep your chopsticks off the table and dry after wash.
How to print: No supports needed. Print vertical on the bed for best results.

Note: This design is intended for standard chopsticks. If you have unusually large or small chopsticks, you may need to adjust the dimensions accordingly.
